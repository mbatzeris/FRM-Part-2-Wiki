## Key Themes
- **VaR Quantification:** Historical Simulation vs. Parametric vs. EVT.
- **Model Validation:** Coverage tests, PIT, and Regulatory (Basel) standards.
- **Correlation & Dependency:** Copulas, Correlation Swaps, and PCA.
- **Term Structure:** Drift/Volatility modeling and No-arbitrage conditions.
- **FRTB:** Expected Shortfall, SBA, and Internal Models Approach.
